### **论文写作建议：如何描述“获取策略”**

根据 Source 和 (Python 数学实验书)，在论文的 _Parameter Estimation_ 章节，请使用以下专业表述：

1. **对于拟合参数 ($V_{oc}$ coefficients)**:
    
    > "To capture the non-linear relationship between Open Circuit Voltage (OCV) and SOC, we utilized the **NASA Battery Prognostics Dataset** [Ref]. Using the `numpy.polyfit` module in Python, we performed a **least-squares polynomial fitting** of the 3rd order. The resulting coefficient of determination ($R^2$) was 0.99, indicating a high fidelity to the experimental data." _(此处展示一张拟合曲线图，散点为数据，实线为你的多项式)_
    
2. **对于推导参数 ($k_{ref}$)**:
    
    > "Rather than assigning an arbitrary value for the aging rate, we **derived** $k_{ref}$ from the industry-standard lifespan metric (80% SOH after 500 cycles). Assuming a geometric decay process, we solved the equation $(1-k_{ref})^{500} = 0.8$."
    
3. **对于随机参数 ($\gamma(t)$)**:
    
    > "To simulate realistic signal fluctuations, we modeled $\gamma(t)$ as an **Ornstein-Uhlenbeck process**. The parameters $\theta$ and $\sigma$ were estimated to reflect the typical time-correlation of signal strength observed in urban environments."
    

这张表格和相应的描述策略将完美契合 Source 中关于 "Data as support, not substitute" 的要求，证明你的模型是基于扎实的物理和数据基础，而非空中楼阁。
