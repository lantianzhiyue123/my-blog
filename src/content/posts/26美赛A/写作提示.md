### 拿到结果后如何写进论文？

在论文的 **“Parameter Estimation（参数估计）”** 章节，你可以这样描述：

> "To avoid arbitrary parameter assignment for temperature sensitivity, we estimated the Activation Energy ($E_a$) by cross-referencing the **NASA PCoE Battery Dataset**. We extracted electrochemical impedance spectroscopy (EIS) data from **Battery #5 ($24^\circ C$)** and **Battery #41 ($4^\circ C$)**. By averaging the sum of electrolyte resistance ($R_e$) and charge-transfer resistance ($R_{ct}$) over the initial cycles, we observed a significant resistance increase from $R_{297K} \approx \dots \Omega$ to $R_{277K} \approx \dots \Omega$. Applying the Arrhenius relationship, we derived $E_a \approx \dots \text{ J/mol}$, which serves as the physical basis for our temperature-dependent resistance model."
> 
> +1

这样写，你的论文在“数据支撑”和“物理推理”这两项评价指标上都会拿到极高的分数
